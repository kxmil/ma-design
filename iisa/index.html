In this project I am looking into my familyâ€™s Karelian heritage and the transmitting and disappearing of cultural information through generations, especially regarding the history of my grandparents refuging from Karelia. I am exploring this subject through archival images, interviews of my family and material experimentation in the form of wearable artifacts.

Through material I explore, arrange and let myself be guided by the information the material gives me. For the moment I don't have a specific expectation about the final product, but there are many methods and techniques that I would like to start with and see where they take me.

Right now I am working on a piece of jewellery: a necklace that I am designing on a cast made after my chest. After this I will move on to textile experiments with printing and distressing with laser cutting. All of this is organized around a collection of old photographs.

As this is a personal theme with many sensitive and even political layers, I want to approach it with care and time. I'm planning to continue this project in Finland and see this semester only as a starting point.
