MA DESIGN

The MA Design: Explorations & Translations brings together multiple practices: design, media studies, and sciences. Using the dynamic landscapes and industries of Iceland as an experimental ground,the student cohort develops unique interdisciplinary voices in the design field. In parallel with thorough research mediated by a Thesis and collective publications, the students materialise and manifest design proposals through a range of media such as: text, video works, web based media and XR. Using a combination of applied and narrative design tools, the emphasis is on critical thinking and the student´s authorship of their project.
